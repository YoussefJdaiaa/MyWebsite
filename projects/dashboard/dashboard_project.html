Project Details

Development of an interactive dashboard to analyze electric vehicles in the state of Washington. Utilization of modern technologies such as Plotly, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to create dynamic charts. Visualization of data on vehicle distribution by year, market shares of brands, and average vehicle range.
